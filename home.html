Emergency care is a responsibility of primary care doctors. A medical emergency is an injury or illness that poses an immediate risk to a person's life or long-term health and it's extremely important to attend to these patients immediately. The early identification of a medical emergency and subsequent management will be helpful to save the patient's life and prevent irreversible consequences.
